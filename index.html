  • ~5% of children & ~4% of adults suffer from ADHD
  • Over 80% have not been diagnosed
  • Demand for assessments grown exponentially since 2020
  • ADHD assessments are complicated and time-consuming
  • Hard to get treatment - Years of waiting (NHS), expensive (private)
  • Untreated ADHD = ¬£135K economic burden per patient
